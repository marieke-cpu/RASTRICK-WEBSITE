§ 02 / The Story

How I got here.

Origin story

Design
found me
first.

It started with noticing how things work. The way a landing page communicates trust before anyone reads a word. The gap between a site that converts and one that just exists — and why that gap is worth more to a business than any aesthetic upgrade.

"I wanted to build things that didn't just look good — they had to work."

Strategy came first. Then design. Then code — when I realised that handing off a design meant losing control of the details that drive results. The heading hierarchy that guides a visitor's eye. The form placement that reduces friction. The mobile experience that most builders ignore. I needed to own the outcome end to end.

So I learned how to build what I design. Not from a shortcut framework — from understanding how digital experiences are actually constructed, what makes one convert better than another, and how to align every decision with a measurable business goal.

The result is a practice built around outcomes. Not just design, not just development — but the combination of strategy, conversion thinking, and execution that turns a website into a business asset. Matched to the right platform for every client. Every time.

That's the studio now. Strategy-led design, built to perform from day one. One point of contact. No handoff gap. No details lost between the brief and the launch.

§ 03 / Philosophy

Performance first.
Always.

Most businesses don't need the same platform. They need the right one. Every RASTRICK project starts with platform selection — matched to your goals, budget, timeline, and growth trajectory. That is what makes builds launch faster and perform better.

Wix for fast, cost-efficient launches. Webflow for scale-ready design and flexibility. Shopify for eCommerce that converts. Custom code when performance and control must be absolute. The platform serves the strategy. Always.

Every build is structured around conversion clarity, page speed, and clean user flow. The focus is on what the site actually does for the business — more enquiries, more sales, better performance — not how it looks in a screenshot. Outcomes over aesthetics. Every time.

One point of contact throughout. The person defining strategy is the same person designing, building, and refining the result. No layers. No handoff confusion. Execution stays aligned with intent from start to finish.
