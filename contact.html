§ 04 / Before you ask

Common questions.

Q / 01

How quickly will you reply?

Within one business day, always — usually faster. Every message goes directly to me. No support tickets. No bots. No waiting on hold.

Q / 02

Do I need a full brief ready?

No. Even a rough idea is enough to start. Just tell me your goals and what you want the site to achieve — I'll help figure out the platform, scope, and right build path. We work through the details together.

Q / 03

What's the Kickstart offer?

Kickstart is a reduced-rate package on Wix, exclusively for small businesses and startups who need a proper, conversion-focused website but aren't at full package budget yet. Same strategic thinking. Same design quality. Built to fit where you are right now.

Q / 04

How long does a project take?

Starter and Kickstart builds typically go live in 1–2 weeks. Multi-page Growth or eCommerce builds take 3–4 weeks. Custom builds vary by scope. Every project gets a clear timeline before work begins.

Q / 05

Do you work remotely?

Yes — entirely. Based in Canberra, working with businesses across Australia and internationally. Everything happens online: calls, feedback, file delivery, and full platform handover.

Q / 06

What if I'm not ready yet?

Still get in touch. I'll recommend the right platform and package and we can map out a realistic timeline together. No pressure — the right build starts when the timing is actually right for your business.
