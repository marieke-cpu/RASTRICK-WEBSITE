§ 01 / Category

The process.

How projects run from first message to launch, with clear decisions at every step.
01How does the process work from start to launch?+

It starts with your goal: more leads, more sales, or better conversion from existing traffic. I reply within one business day. From there:

  • Strategy — goals, audience, offer, and traffic source are defined
  • Platform fit — Wix, Webflow, Shopify, or Custom chosen for your use case
  • Scope — fixed scope, timeline, and fixed investment
  • Design — conversion-focused page structure and visual direction
  • Build — implementation with speed, responsiveness, and tracking
  • Launch — QA, performance checks, and go-live setup
  • Optimise — post-launch support and next-step recommendations
02Do I need a full brief before reaching out?+

No. A rough outline is enough. If you can share what you sell, who you sell to, and what result you want from the site, that is plenty to begin.

I’ll help turn that into a clear scope and the right build path. No polished brief required.

03What do you need from me to start?+

Once scope is approved and deposit is paid, I need:

  • Core business information and your main offer
  • Brand assets (logo, colors, fonts) if available
  • Content and imagery, or direction for sourcing
  • Any current ad campaigns or traffic priorities
  • Domain/hosting or platform access when launch is due

If anything is missing, we map it in the kickoff plan.

04How do revisions work?+

Each package includes a set number of revision rounds. Feedback is consolidated into one list per round, then implemented in full.

This keeps delivery fast and prevents project drag. Additional rounds can be scoped in if needed.

05Do I approve design before build starts?+

Yes. Design direction and page structure are approved first. Build starts only after sign-off so execution is aligned with your goals.

§ 02 / Category

Tech & code.

How platform choice and technical decisions affect conversion, speed, and scalability.
06Do you only build custom-coded websites?+

No. RASTRICK builds on Wix, Webflow, Shopify, and custom code.

The platform is selected based on business goals, speed-to-launch, budget, and required flexibility. One stack does not fit every business.

07How do you choose the right platform?+

Platform choice is based on commercial fit:

  • Wix — fast and cost-effective launch
  • Webflow — scalable marketing websites
  • Shopify — eCommerce and conversion flow
  • Custom — advanced performance and flexibility
08Can you build landing pages for paid ads?+

Yes. Ad landing pages are built around message match, offer clarity, mobile speed, and low-friction enquiry or checkout flow.

They are designed to improve conversion rate from paid traffic, not just look polished.

09Will my website be mobile-optimised?+

Yes. Every build is mobile-first because most users and ad clicks come from mobile devices.

Layouts, forms, and CTAs are tested for speed and usability before launch.

10What devices and browsers are tested?+

Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge across desktop and mobile. iOS Safari and Android Chrome are treated as critical checks.

§ 03 / Category

Packages & pricing.

How investment is scoped, how platform choice affects delivery, and what each package is built for.
11How are projects priced?+

Projects are fixed-price. You receive clear scope, timeline, deliverables, and investment before work begins.

If scope changes, it is quoted and approved before implementation.

12Why isn't there a one-size pricing list?+

Because project needs vary by platform, complexity, and commercial goal.

A fast Wix launch and a custom high-performance build are different jobs. Pricing reflects fit and outcome.

13What are your payment terms?+

A deposit secures your slot and starts production. Remaining payments are milestone-based and documented in your proposal.

14Which package is right for my business?+

Quick guide:

  • Launchpad — fast Wix launch for early-stage businesses
  • The Build — Webflow growth site for lead generation
  • Signalfire — Shopify-focused conversion stack for eCommerce
  • The Studio — premium custom build for advanced needs
  • Kickstart — reduced-rate option for eligible early-stage operators

If you're unsure, I’ll recommend the best fit based on goals, not upsell targets.

15Can we start smaller and scale later?+

Yes. Projects are structured to scale. You can launch lean, then add pages, funnels, products, or features in planned phases.

§ 04 / Category

Timelines.

Delivery windows by package and what affects turnaround.
16How long does a project usually take?+

As a general guide:

  • Launchpad / Kickstart — 1 to 2 weeks
  • The Build — 2 to 4 weeks
  • Signalfire — 3 to 5 weeks
  • The Studio — 4 to 8 weeks

Timing depends on scope readiness, feedback speed, and integration complexity.

17Can you launch quickly if we have a deadline?+

Yes, where scope allows. If content and approvals are ready, fast-turnaround launches are possible.

18What causes delays?+

Most delays come from incomplete content, slow feedback, or mid-project scope changes.

  • Late content or asset delivery
  • Delayed decision-making on key pages
  • Access delays for domains, platforms, or integrations

Clear kickoff and milestone approvals reduce this risk significantly.

19Do you offer priority delivery?+

Yes, when capacity allows. Priority delivery is scoped around deadline, complexity, and readiness.

SEO & performance.

How technical SEO and site speed are handled across different platform builds.
20Do you include SEO foundations?+

Yes. Every build includes technical SEO fundamentals:

  • Search-friendly page structure and metadata
  • Clean URLs and image alt text

Fast by default. Performance targets are set during scope and validated before launch.

§ 06 / Category

Ownership & handover.

What you own after launch, how updates work, and what handover includes.
23Who owns the website or store after launch?+

You do. Access and ownership are transferred at handover based on your platform setup.

You are not locked into ongoing management retainers unless you choose support.

24Can my team update content after launch?+

Yes. Wix, Webflow, and Shopify builds are straightforward to update in-house.

Custom builds can be handed over with documentation or managed through support if preferred.

25What is included in handover?+

At handover you receive:

  • Platform/account access and key credentials
  • Assets and implementation files relevant to your build
  • Tracking and integration status
  • Clear next-step and support guidance

Everything is documented so your team can operate confidently.

26Are there ongoing monthly fees to RASTRICK?+

No mandatory monthly fee to RASTRICK. Ongoing costs are typically platform, hosting, apps, or domain fees based on your setup.

§ 08 / Category

Kickstart.

How the Kickstart option works for early-stage businesses.
31What is the Kickstart package?+

Kickstart is a reduced-rate option for qualified early-stage businesses that need a credible launch website without full-package investment.

It is scoped to essentials so you can go live quickly and upgrade cleanly later.

32Who is Kickstart designed for?+

Kickstart is for early-stage operators such as:

  • Sole traders and service businesses launching properly
  • Startups validating offers and demand
  • Founders running ads who need a conversion-ready destination
  • Teams with constrained launch budgets

If your scope is broader, another package will be a better fit.

33How do I apply for Kickstart?+

Use the contact form and select Kickstart. Share your business stage, offer, and launch goal.

You’ll get a fit assessment and next-step recommendation within one business day.

34How many Kickstart spots are available?+

Kickstart spots are capped each quarter to protect quality and delivery speed.

If the current intake is full, the next available window is provided up front.

35Can I upgrade from Kickstart later?+

Yes. Kickstart is built as a launch foundation so you can expand into broader packages without starting over.
