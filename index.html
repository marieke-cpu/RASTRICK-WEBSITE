§ 05 / FAQ

Questions, answered.

How long does a project take?+
Most landing pages ship in 2 weeks. A full brand + site takes 4 weeks end to end, depending on how many rounds of feedback we need and how quickly content is supplied.
Do you code the sites yourself?+
Depends on what site you need. If, it is a basic site for service based buinesses, then I do not use code. For more complex sites I hand-coded in HTML, CSS and JavaScript — no page builders, no WordPress, no templates. You get clean, fast, custom code that you actually own.
What do you need from me to start?+
A deposit, your brand assets (logo, colours, fonts if you have them), any copy or content you want on the site, and a clear idea of what you want it to do. No brief is too rough — we can work through it together on a discovery call.
Do you work with businesses outside Australia?+
Absolutely. Yes. I'm based in Canberra but work with clients remotely across Australia and internationally. Everything is handled online — calls, feedback, and delivery.
What if I only need a redesign, not a full rebrand?+
That's what Signalfire is for. If your brand is already strong and you just need a site that actually converts, it's the right fit. We can also scope a custom package if your needs fall outside the standard three.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?+
Yes. Every package includes post-launch support. After that window, I offer monthly care plans for updates, new pages, ad variants, and performance monitoring.
