§ 05 / FAQ

Questions, answered.

How long does a project take?+
A single-page site typically ships in 1–2 weeks. A multi-page build takes 3–6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Timelines depend on how quickly content is supplied and the number of revision rounds we go through.
Which platform is best for my project?+
It depends on your goals, timeline, and budget. Wix is great for fast launches, Webflow suits growth-focused service brands, Shopify is best for eCommerce, and custom builds are ideal when performance and flexibility matter most. I’ll recommend the right fit, not a one-size-fits-all stack.
Do you only build custom-coded websites?+
No. RASTRICK. MADE builds on Wix, Webflow, Shopify, and custom code. The goal is performance and conversion, so the build method is selected based on what will produce the best business result for your case.
Can you build for paid ads and campaign traffic?+
Yes. Landing pages and conversion flow are built specifically for ad traffic quality. Message match, CTA hierarchy, mobile speed, and enquiry flow are all structured to lift lead and sales outcomes.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?+
Absolutely. Every website I build is mobile-first and tested across devices and screen sizes. A site that doesn't work perfectly on a phone isn't finished — that's not how I ship.
What do you need from me to start?+
A deposit, any existing brand assets (logo, colours, fonts if you have them), content or copy you want on the site, and a clear idea of what you want it to achieve. No brief is too rough — we'll work through it together on a discovery call.

Ready for a site that drives more leads and sales?
