Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit this website or get in touch with us.

Last updated: May 2026

01 — Introduction

Who we are

RASTRICK. MADE is an independent website design and development studio based in Canberra, ACT, Australia. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information collected through this website (rastrick.com.au) and through enquiries submitted via our contact form.

By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you have any questions, contact us at hello@rastrick.com.au.

02 — Information We Collect

Personal information

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name and details
  • The content of any message you send us
  • Any other information you choose to include in your enquiry

Usage data

We also collect anonymous, non-personally identifiable data about how visitors interact with this website via Google Analytics (see Section 04). This data does not identify you as an individual.

03 — How We Use Your Information

Purpose of collection

Information you submit is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your enquiry or project brief
  • Providing the services you have requested or enquired about
  • Sending follow-up communication related to your enquiry
  • Improving the overall experience of this website

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing.

04 — Google Analytics

Analytics and tracking

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics collects anonymous data about how visitors use this site — including pages visited, time spent, browser type, and general geographic region. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and report this information. The data collected is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

05 — Cookies

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function efficiently and to provide reporting information to site owners.

How we use cookies

This website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies track anonymous usage data to help us understand how visitors interact with the site
  • Performance: Session cookies may be used to remember your preferences during a single visit (such as skipping the intro animation on return visits)

Managing cookies

You can control and disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of this website.

For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

06 — Data Protection

How we protect your data

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information you provide against loss, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for, or as required by applicable law.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Access and control

You have the right to request access to any personal information we hold about you, request that we correct or delete it, or withdraw consent for us to use it. To make any such request, contact us at hello@rastrick.com.au.
