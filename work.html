§ 03 / Background

Where it started.

The origin

A family
business with
big ambitions.

Angelo and Sharimay started Poly Concreting the way most trades businesses do — hard work, word of mouth, a van and a lot of early mornings. A family-owned concreting company based in Morayfield, Queensland, servicing South-East Queensland with everything from residential driveways to large-scale commercial pours.

The business was growing. The website wasn't keeping up. They were on WIX — the platform many small businesses default to because it's accessible and cheap to get started. But WIX was holding them back. Slow load times. Generic layouts that looked like every other concreting company in Queensland. No real ability to run serious paid-ad campaigns with dedicated landing pages.

"They gave me the opportunity to rebuild their whole website — and I wasn't going to waste it."

Angelo and Sharimay came to RASTRICK ready to take the business seriously online. They were already running Google and Microsoft Ads but sending traffic to a site that wasn't built to convert. The brief was clear: rebuild everything properly, build landers that turn ad clicks into quote requests, and make the site look like the business they were becoming — not the one they started as.

What followed was a complete rebuild. Six custom pages. Four high-converting landing pages. A site built to perform on paid search from day one. Poly Concreting has since broken the $1 million revenue mark. Not because of the website alone — because the founders are exceptional operators. But the website stopped being a liability and started being an asset.

§ 04 / Problem & approach

The challenge. The fix.

The problem

What WIX couldn't do.

WIX had gotten Poly Concreting online. But it was generating slow, bloated pages that couldn't convert ad traffic, couldn't rank competitively on local SEO, and looked identical to thousands of other tradesman websites built on the same platform.

Sending paid ad traffic to a WIX site is like filling a leaky bucket. You're paying for clicks but losing them at the landing page because the page isn't built to do one thing well. Generalist pages don't convert. They just exist.

  • Slow page load on mobile — the majority of their traffic
  • No dedicated landing pages — ad traffic going to a generic home page
  • No location-specific pages for regional SEO
  • Template-driven design identical to competitor sites
  • No trust-building architecture — no social proof, no urgency, no clear CTA flow
  • Code bloat from WIX's builder generating hundreds of unused elements
The approach

Built to convert.

The rebuild started from a blank file. Not a WIX export, not a theme — nothing. Every page was designed for a specific purpose and built to do exactly that one thing well.

The main site needed to establish trust quickly and funnel visitors toward a quote request. The landing pages needed to do one thing only: convert a qualified ad click into a lead. No navigation to distract, no unrelated content, no reason to leave without filling in the form.

  • Hand-coded HTML, CSS & JS — no builder overhead
  • Mobile-first build — designed for phones before desktop
  • 4 dedicated landing pages built specifically for paid campaigns
  • Strong trust signals: reviews, credentials, project photos, local area focus
  • Clear conversion architecture on every page — one goal, one CTA
  • Regional targeting built into landing page copy and structure for local SEO

§ 06 / Ad landing pages

Built to convert clicks.

Landing page / 01 · Google Ads

Commercial Concreting

Purpose-built for Poly Concreting's commercial Google Ads campaign. Every element on this page has one job: turn a click from a commercial building or construction manager into a qualified lead. No main site navigation. No distractions. Just a compelling argument for why Poly Concreting is the right choice for commercial jobs in South-East Queensland.

The page targets commercial concreting keywords — slab work, warehouse floors, car parks, commercial driveways. Built with a strong hero, credibility stack, project examples from commercial jobs, and a prominent quote request form that stays visible as you scroll.

Conversion architecture

  • No main nav — traffic stays on the page
  • Hero with clear commercial-focused headline
  • Services specific to commercial jobs
  • Project gallery filtered to commercial work
  • Google Reviews integration — trust from real customers
  • Sticky quote CTA visible throughout scroll
  • Click-to-call button prominent on mobile
  • Fast load — no WIX overhead on paid traffic

Targeting

  • Google Ads — commercial concreting keywords
  • South-East Queensland targeting
  • High-intent search terms: "commercial concreter QLD", "concrete slab contractor Brisbane"
Landing page / 02 · Microsoft Ads

Moreton Bay Services

Regional lander built specifically for Microsoft (Bing) Ads targeting the Moreton Bay region — Morayfield, Caboolture, Narangba, Burpengary, North Lakes and surrounds. Hyper-local trust signals matter here — the page makes it clear that Poly Concreting are locals who know the area and have done jobs right in the visitor's suburb.

Microsoft Ads captures a different audience to Google — often older demographics, homeowners, established businesses. The page tone and layout were tuned for that. Straightforward, reassuring, and focused on the quote request form as the single conversion goal.
